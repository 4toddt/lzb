La-Z-Boy Unveils a New Look for High Point Market Showroom

HIGH POINT, N.C., (October 13, 2012) – La-Z-Boy, the leader in home furnishings, is excited to debut its beautifully renovated showroom at the fall 2012 High Point Furniture Market.

Like so many La-Z-Boy products, the new and improved showroom combines function with style. Within the space, walls have been removed to provide guests with better sight lines and access to all furniture displays, and each vignette is emphasized by newly installed energy efficient LED lighting. The result is a new furniture shopping environment perfectly in keeping with the fast evolving home furnishings brand.

In addition to new design features, the architectural layout of the space will be enhanced with a completely renovated dealer lounge. A new concept design center has also been created on the upper level to help buyers work with and visualize how La-Z-Boy’s over 800 fabrics and leathers create complete rooms that fits any style.

“We are excited to show our guests a full line of great looking furniture in our beautiful showroom … we know they will be impressed by all they see,” said Paula Hoyas. “We put as much time and effort into this design as we do into each piece of new furniture we produce, ensuring we stay on the cutting edge of style.”

All of the latest La-Z-Boy furniture will be on display to the trade October 13th through October 18th at the La-Z-Boy showroom located in the Furniture Plaza Building in High Point, N.C. The furniture will be available at La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries?, La-Z-Boy Comfort Studio® dealers and anywhere genuine La-Z-Boy furniture is sold. Customers can visit www.La-Z-Boy.com to find the retailer nearest them.

About La-Z-Boy

Headquartered in Monroe, Mich., La-Z-Boy has been producing some of the world’s most comfortable furniture for over 85 years. The company manufactures a full line of comfortable products for the living room and family room, including the company’s world-famous recliners, reclining sofas and love seats, sleep sofas, modular furniture and leather upholstery, as well as stationary sofas, love seats and chairs. It is a division of La-Z-Boy Incorporated (NYSE: LZB), one of the world’s leading residential furniture producers, marketing furniture for every room of the home. "Live Life Comfortably.®"
