La-Z-Boy Enhances Digital Platforms to Ensure Comfortable Online Experience for Consumers

Provides a one-stop shopping destination regardless of device

MONROE, Mich., (January 16, 2013) – As time spent on mobile devices, tablets and other digital platforms continues to increase, La-Z-Boy is leading the way, ensuring consumers have what they need, when they need it and in the format that works best for them.

In January, La-Z-Boy will be introducing the second version of its mobile platform that will include zip-code based pricing to ensure consumers have accurate, local information at their fingertips, and the ability to customize furniture with any fabric (currently available online at www.la-z-boy.com). Consumers will also have access to their nearest store with contact information and directions based on geo-locating information.

The new mobile site was designed around a La-Z-Boy usability study that measured ease of navigation, aesthetics, functional performance and overall likes and dislikes.

"Consumers want digital capabilities available on their smartphone in an efficient and seamless way that suits the platform," said Matt Targett, Director, Interactive and Product Marketing at La-Z-Boy. "La-Z-Boy is well positioned to capitalize on this trend and believes that having the ability to do everything from selecting fabrics to locating a store on a mobile device will ensure the consumer has a positive experience on our site."

Also new to the La-Z-Boy site is the Style Finder feature, giving consumers the opportunity to identify their style by completing an engaging, yet short quiz. With the wide selection of great-looking furniture that La-Z-Boy offers and the hundreds of fabrics available, the Style Finder helps consumers create the room of their dreams.

Additionally, the company now offers a French Canadian and Spanish website that allows La-Z-Boy to create brand awareness and communicate with a new consumer base.

"La-Z-Boy is focused on ensuring that our website and all of our other digital platforms are destinations that inspire consumers, educate them on our products and where possible, allow them to purchase the perfect pieces for their home," said Targett.

About La-Z-Boy

Headquartered in Monroe, Mich., La-Z-Boy has been producing some of the world’s most comfortable furniture for over 85 years.
