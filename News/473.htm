La-Z-Boy Expands On Style, Comfort With New Additions to Its Most-Popular Collections

HIGH POINT, N.C., (Sept. 17, 2014) – With the introduction of the Urban Attitudes collection at High Point Market last October, La-Z-Boy’s commitment to sophisticated, modern offerings has never been more apparent. La-Z-Boy is building on its momentum with the addition of 10 new products across its line, including its Urban Attitudes and Designer’s Choice collections, all while staying true to its history as a company rooted in comfort and quality for more than 85 years.

“The new items we are adding to each of our collections this year build on our selection of furniture pieces with clean, simple lines and details that pop for today’s style-conscious demographic,” said Paula Hoyas, vice president of merchandising for La-Z-Boy. “From sofas to chairs and ottomans, each look can be customized to fit into any room in the house.”

Featuring beautiful textures, rich colors and bold patterns, each addition flawlessly blends classic features with modern settings to make a stylish statement in any setting. And with more than 75 new fabrics added to La-Z- Boy’s library of more than 800 cover options, consumers will be able to create the perfect piece that matches their personal style.

All of the latest La-Z-Boy selections will be on display Oct. 18 - 23 at the La-Z-Boy showroom located in Space 103 of the Furniture Plaza Building in High Point, North Carolina. The furniture will be available soon at La-Z- Boy Furniture Galleries®, La-Z-Boy Comfort Studios® and anywhere genuine La-Z-Boy furniture is sold.

New to the Urban Attitudes Collection
La-Z-Boy’s Urban Attitudes collection of premium sofas, chairs and accents is perfectly proportioned for urban living spaces. This fall, La-Z-Boy expands the collection with the introduction of three new products.

  • Uptown Stationary Sofa and “Chofa”

    Uptown features clean, contemporary styling with a unique, stacked-track arm, single-needle topstitching accents and new decorative legs with a metal cap. This sofa is also available as a “chofa” (sofa with reversible chaise ottoman/cushion) offering fun and versatile functionality fit for the big city and small-space living.

  • Gridiron Stationary Chair and Ottoman

    Referred to as the chameleon of the Urban Attitudes collection, Gridiron’s wood accent offering carries a casual groove with a sophisticated flare, making it perfectly at ease in both formal and relaxed settings. The decorative wood back, arms and legs create a nice pairing for nearly any fabric, and its matching ottoman provides extra comfort for the end of a long commute.

  • Midtown Swivel Chair

    The Midtown Swivel uses simple, clean lines to meet the criteria for a modern living space. Along with its swivel capabilities, the chair features a tight back, loose box-border seat and slightly curved arms accented with welt trim, making it a standout furniture piece.

More New Stationary Pieces
La-Z-Boy expands its line of stationary sofas with two traditional pieces available in a variety of fabrics for all tastes and styles.

  • Leighton Stationary Sofa

    An update on the traditional petite roll-arm stationary sofa, the Leighton features a decorative turned leg and optional nail head trim. The welt trim extends to the sofa’s back, seats, arm posts and accent pillows, and its style is ideal for those who prefer linen or linen-like covers and neutral colors or an updated casual, traditional flair.

  • Brennan Stationary Sofa

    The Brennan features petite decorative nailhead trim along its contemporized, collared back and arm posts, giving a subtle outline to its silhouette, while larger, spaced-out nailhead along the bottom front rail provides drama. Loose T-seat cushions with generous depth and semi-attached backs add to Brennan’s comfort while three wooden bun feet complement transitional styling.

New to the Signature Leather Line
La-Z-Boy brings several new additions to its Signature Leather line, all of which combine stylish frames with some of its most popular leather colors.

  • Channing Sofa

    The Channing sofa brings old world flair in a classic Chesterfield look, featuring a plush, tufted back and shelter arms. The tight seat cushions provide the look of a bench style seat, while the two-tone, buttery soft leather freshens this traditional style in tones of charcoal and coffee.

  • Julius Stationary Chair

    Casual, updated, yet traditional, Julius features a pub-style back highlighted with single-needle, topstitched detailing and a petite roll arm for classic simplicity.

  • Graham Sectional

    Building on the success of the Graham sofa introduction from April, new sectional pieces have been added, allowing for multiple configurations with this high-style silhouette featuring curvaceous arms and semi-attached backs.

  • Merry Single Swivel Chair

    The Merry, a single swivel chair, is a new spin on the iconic barrel chair with an appealing shape that features curvaceous scooped arms and a polished metal base.

New to the Designer’s Choice Collection
For modern, style-savvy consumers, La-Z-Boy’s Designer’s Choice collection offers: the Carleton, as well as the Gridiron Stationary Chair and Ottoman and the Midtown Swivel, which are also a part of the Urban Attitudes Collection.

  • Carleton Push-Back Recliner

    Inspired by the leather Tarleton High Leg Recliner, the Carleton matches the scale and comfort of its predecessor while offering hundreds of fabric choices, allowing consumers to customize this classic look to blend flawlessly into the living room.
