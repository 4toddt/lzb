La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries Sales Associates And In-Home Designers Recognized For Achievements

MONROE, Mich., (April 25, 2014) - La-Z-Boy recently announced the recipients of its Pinnacle Awards during a ceremony at the April Market in High Point, N.C. The award recognizes the top-selling sales associates and highest-achieving in-home designers in the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries network.
“Our Pinnacle Awards truly acknowledge our associates’ commitment to helping bring our brand to life in the area of consumer experience,” said Mark Bacon, President of La-Z-Boy Branded Business. “We applaud these individuals and are honored to recognize them for the success they had throughout the year.”
The Pinnacle Award honors outstanding sales consultants in each of the company's five sales regions in North America.
The 2013 La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries sales associate recipients include:

  • Harriet DiGiovanni of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Monmouth Junction, NJ  
  • Brandi Loring of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Davenport, IA
  • JoAnn Davis of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Pleasant Hill, CA  
  • Roshan Chugani of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Raleigh, NC
  • Steve Williamson of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Calgary, Alberta

The 2013 La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in-home designer recipients include:

  • Whitney Campbell of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Newark, DE  
  • Kathryn Czuchra of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Novi, MI
  • Linda Jackley of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Glendale, Ariz.  
  • Gil Croy of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Pineville, NC
  • Vivian Jost of the La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries in Richmond, British Columbia  

About La-Z-Boy

Headquartered in Monroe, Mich., La-Z-Boy has been producing some of the world’s most comfortable furniture for over 85 years. The company manufactures a full line of comfortable products for the living room and family room, including the company’s world-famous recliners, reclining sofas and love seats, sleep sofas, modular furniture and leather upholstery, as well as stationary sofas, love seats and chairs. It is a division of La-Z-Boy Incorporated (NYSE: LZB), one of the world’s leading residential furniture producers, marketing furniture for every room of the home. "Live Life Comfortably.®"
