How should I clean my Outdoor Furniture?

With some simple cleaning, you can maintain the attractive appearance of your furniture.

  • Frames

    • Wash with a solution of mild soap and water. Rinse with clean water and dry with a soft, absorbent cloth towel. For maximum protection against UV rays and/or salty air, frames can be treated with a liquid wax.

  • Cushions

    • Clean by hand with a solution of soap and water and rinse with water. After cleaning, tip cushions on end and allow to drip dry. Never machine wash, machine dry or dry clean cushions. Please note: any use of bleach or cleaning solvents will void the warranty.
