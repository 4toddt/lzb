We are here to help

A message from your friends at La-Z-Boy

From all of us at La-Z-Boy, we want to sincerely thank you for being part of the nationwide effort to keep our local communities healthy and safe. We are especially grateful for our first responders and healthcare professionals who are heroically working to help those in need. We appreciate your patience and understanding as we navigate through these challenging times.

At present time, we have made the decision to temporarily close most of our retail showrooms in order to preserve the health and safety of our communities. This is in accordance with government directives to bring the spread of COVID-19 under control as quickly as possible.

Although most of our retail stores are closed, our online store at www.la-z-boy.com is still open and accepting new orders and many of our interior designers are available for virtual consultations. As much as possible, our warehouse and delivery services are up and running, while observing enhanced health protocols to help keep you and our team members safe.

We look forward to bringing you all the style, value and comfort you expect. If you need to reach us for any reason, our Comfort Care team is available (working remotely) and is here to help via email or phone.

Thank you for choosing La-Z-Boy to help create a home you love. Take care of yourself and your loved ones, and we hope to see you soon.

Your La-Z-Boy Family

Questions that may be on your mind.

Why are you closing your retail stores at a time when we need comfort more than ever?

Like you, we are following rules established by local governments to prevent the spread of COVID-19 and flatten the curve. At present time, we have made the decision to temporarily close most of our retail showrooms. Our primary focus is the health, safety and well-being of our employees and their families, as well as our customers and the communities in which we operate.

Are all your stores closed?

We are following the guidance of local governments, the Centers for Disease Control and the World Health Organization. It’s too soon to tell when all our stores may reopen but we encourage you to check back on La-Z-Boy.com for updates. Once our stores are open, we will continue to ensure the health and safety of our customers and employees. This includes additional procedures to clean and disinfect surfaces within our stores.

What if I still want to buy something? Please can I?

Good news. The nice people who work on La-Z-Boy.com are keeping things going during these uncertain times. You can still find inspiration, research products, even see what products look like in our own home. Sound too good to be true? Download the La-Z-Boy Augmented Reality App and see for yourself.

Are you still delivering orders in those good looking La-Z-Boy trucks?

Our warehouse and delivery services are up and running where possible while observing enhanced health protocols to help keep you and our team members safe. Preventing the spread of COVID-19 is our first priority. Therefore, we have made the decision to temporarily suspend in-home delivery services in many locations.

While your stores are closed should I expect a delay in my order?

Where delivery is available, many of our products can be ready for you to enjoy in a matter of days. In other cases we will custom build your new product and have it to your home in as little as 6-8 weeks.

What precautions are you taking when providing in-home delivery?

Our delivery drivers are taking additional measures to keep everyone safe. We are following guidelines provided by the Centers for Disease Control including cleaning and disinfecting surfaces you may come into contact with during the delivery process. We may also keep our distance while in your home and we encourage you to do the same.

I love La-Z-Boy, but I’d really like to wait and have my order delivered at a later date.

No problem, we get it. We want you to feel safe, healthy and comfortable in your new La-Z-Boy product. If you would like to take delivery at a later date we are happy to reschedule for you. Just give us a call and we will find a date and time that you are comfortable with.

Are interior designers still available to transform my room from drab to fab?

Where stores are closed our interior designers may be unable to offer in-home design consultations. As much as possible, our designers are still doing their best to provide you with advice, ideas and recommendations. In some locations, we are also offering virtual design services. Click Here to learn about our Free Design Services, see some examples of the magic we work and request to speak with a designer.

How can I remake my room when I’m being careful not to leave my home?

We live by the rule – “when life gets tough, rearrange the furniture.” So, we have created a number of tools and services that make it easy to transform your home, while at home. Click Here to request free swatches samples delivered direct to your door. To customize furniture and see what it will look like in your own home, download our Augmented Reality app for iOS. To transform your entire room, or your entire house, we recommend our 3D Room Planner.
