Jenni Radosevich

I Spy DIY

Growing up in Wisconsin, Jenni was always creating her own style. If she saw something in a magazine that she couldn’t find at the store, she would make it herself. After moving to New York City, Jenni started a career at InStyle magazine, working as a graphic designer and DIY columnist, which evolved into a website: ISpyDIY.com and book, I Spy DIY Style, and product line #ispydiysupplies. Jenni has moved back to Milwaukee, where she is renovating homes (one flip on HGTV!) and continues to create and grow I Spy DIY.
