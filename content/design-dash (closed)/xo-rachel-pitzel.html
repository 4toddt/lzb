Rachel is a digital content creator focusing on fashion, beauty, travel and wellness based in Los Angeles. Grayson and Harper, her two kids, are often in her content which went viral due to her twinning and matching family outfit trend she started in 2016. She loves vibrant pops of color and fun, playful details in her styling. She formerly owned an events based company that she sold to a media company in 2015 and is a reformed attorney.
