A freelance photographer and author of the blog French Country Cottage, Courtney also works as a brand ambassador, designer and author of her first book French Country Cottage. She is a mother of three and lives in a vintage cottage in the California countryside with her husband and adopted dog Sweet Pea - and you can often find her with camera in hand capturing a whimsical moment.
