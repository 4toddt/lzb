Remember, your sofa should allow you to sit comfortably without pillows. The seat cushion should support your hips and thighs, with your knees bent at a 90-degree angle and your feet touching the floor. Of course, the best way to find the perfect fit is to visit your local La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries store and test one in person!
