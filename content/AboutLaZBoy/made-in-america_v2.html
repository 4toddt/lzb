American Heritage

In 1928, the recliner was invented in Monroe, Michigan and La-Z-Boy was born. Our legacy of American ingenuity, drive and pride continues, thanks to the thousands of men and women across the USA who handcraft our products every day with American and imported parts. Together, we’re developing the latest innovations in comfort and convenience, while raising the bar for style and design. It’s what we do.

Real people. Real Americans.

For over 90 years, La-Z-Boy has built the highest-quality furniture, doing much of the work right here in the USA. We employ thousands of men and women across North America who cut, sew, and assemble millions of pieces of furniture each year. They share our vision of craftsmanship, innovation and American heritage, because where and how your furniture is made matters.
