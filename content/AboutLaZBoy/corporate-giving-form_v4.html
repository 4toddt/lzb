Thank you!

Thank you for your interest in La-Z-Boy Incorporated. Although we support many worthwhile programs, your application may be declined because it fell into one of the following categories outside of our eligible criteria:

  • Individual needs (Including emergency relief, or furniture donations)
  • Travel expenses
  • Political organizations
  • Scholarships
  • Fraternities and sororities
  • Personal fundraising initiatives
  • Travel funds for tours
  • Expeditions or trips
  • Beauty contest candidates
  • Mission trips
  • Loans
  • Membership dues
  • Start-up funds or seed money

La-Z-Boy donates through non-profit organizations such as the Ronald McDonald House, United Way, American Red Cross and Relay For Life and not directly to individuals. This enables us to reach many people in need. However, many of our dealers are independently owned and operated and set their own donation policies. Your nearest La-Z-Boy dealer can be located by visiting our website at www.la-z-boy.com and clicking on the “find a dealer” section.

If your request falls under any other category inside our eligible criteria, your application will be reviewed and you will be notified within 1 month of approval. Only applications that are approved will receive communication.
