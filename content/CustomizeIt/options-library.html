The Definition of Comfort

Customize your La-Z-Boy furniture with a wide range of options for style, comfort and convenience.

Note: Not all options are available on all styles

Select your options on the product page.

Cushion Options

  • Airform™ Seat Cushioning

    Upgraded seat and legrest cushion(s) featuring a 2" layer of high-performance airform™ foam over a highly resilient Qualux® foam base.

  • ComfortCore® GEL Seat Cushions

    Upgraded seat cushions consisting of a foam core, plus layers of airform™ and gel-infused foam, wrapped in batting.

Power Options

  • Left Hand Power Activator

    Moves the standard 4-button power controller with USB to the outside arm.

  • Wireless Remote

    A convenient wireless remote control and magnetic cradle with memory and home buttons, mounted on the outside right arm.

  • Left Wireless Remote

    A convenient wireless remote control and magnetic cradle with memory and home buttons, mounted on the outside left arm.

  • Hand Wand

    Replaces the standard 4-button power controller with a hand wand.

  • Power Bundle

    Adds two lighted cupholders, two AC outlets and two USB charging ports to storage consoles.

  • Power Pack

    Adds a rechargeable lithium-ion battery pack to power reclining furniture for cordless operation (not compatible with Power Lift Chairs).

Handle Options

  • Left Side Handle

    The standard wooden handle, moved to the outside left arm.

  • Elongated Handle

    Adds 2" in length to the standard wooden handle.

  • Elongated Left Side Handle

    Adds 2" in length to the standard wooden handle, and moved to the outside left arm.

  • Arc Handle

    Replaces the standard wooden handle with a sleek curved handle in a brushed nickel finish.

  • Arc Left Side Handle

    A sleek curved handle in a brushed nickel finish, moved to the outside left arm.

Base Options

  • Swivel Base

    Adds 360-degree rotation functionality to our Rocking Recliners.

  • Tall Base

    Adds 1.5" to seat height and overall height of our Rocking and Wall Recliners.

Trim Options

  • Nail Head Trim

    A decorative border available in Brass, Platinum or Hammered Black Nickel finishes.

  • No Nail Head Trim

    Removes Nail Head Trim from certain styles on which it is a standard feature.

  • Contrasting Welt

    Changes the welt cord trim on the frames and cushions to a contrasting fabric.

Leg/Finish Options

  • Leg Change

    Replaces the standard chair or sofa leg with an alternate style to suit your decor.

  • Optional Wood Finish

    Changes the finish on exposed wood arms and legs, from standard Brown Mahogany (medium brown) to Coffee (dark brown), Graphite (medium gray) or Driftwood (weathered gray).

Fabric Options

  • Arm Cap / Headrest Cover

    A napkin style piece of fabric measuring 16 ¾" x 16 ¾" that can be used as an arm cap or a headrest cover.

  • Contrasting Fabric

    Available on select styles, this option creates a striking look with two different fabrics on the same piece of furniture.

Pillow Options

  • FeatherLite Comfort Pillows

    Upgraded accent pillows, filled with a supersoft blend of 95% duck feathers and 5% duck down.

  • Contrasting Pillows

    Changes the fabric of accent pillows to a contrasting fabric. (Pillows not included with leather furniture.)

Mattress Options

  • SlumberAir® Mattress System

    Replaces standard sleeper mattress with an upgraded mattress featuring an inflatable air-cushioned top. (Electric air pump included.)

  • Memory Foam Mattress

    Replaces standard sleeper mattress with our upgraded MemoryFoam mattress, made with high-density memory foam that conforms to all body types.
