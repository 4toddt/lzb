Cushion options

Airform™

Airform™ provides responsive cushioning and contoured support so you can enjoy the ultimate in comfort. Available on select La-Z-Boy styles, the 2" layer of airform™ helps reduce pressure with the added benefit of an airform™ layer on the chaise and legrest for softness and support.

Comfortcore gel

Seven layers of comfort cushion technology that includes gel-infused memory foam that provides exceptional support as it conforms to your body.

Recliner options

Personalize your individual comfort

We’re all about giving you choices so that your new furniture doesn’t just suit your home, it suits your way of living. Here’s a list of our options that are available on most products.

Brushed metal handle

With our sleek and ergonomic brushed Arc handle design, reclining is more stylish than ever.

Extra tall base

Our heightened base option adds an additional 1.5" to the seat and overall chair height.

Elongated handle

Our elongated handle is easy to reach and easier to use with an added 2" for effortless reclining.

Swivel base

Enjoy the best of both worlds. Full-reclining comfort and 360° swivel motion.

Extended legrest

Extended legrests are available for most of our non-chaise styles so you can stretch out and relax.

Left handle

We can move the handle to the left side so comfort is always within reach.

Wall recliner

This option lets you kick back and fully recline with the chair placed inches from a wall.

Heat and massage

Available on select recliner styles, heat and massage provide extra comfort at the touch of a button.

Wireless remote

Select recliners are available with a wireless remote, featuring a home button, 2 favorite positions, USB charging port, “Find Me” button and side-mounted magnetic cradle.

Battery pack

Add a battery pack to your recliner and place your furniture anywhere! Already own a power recliner? Visit your local store for more details on how to unplug. Battery pack not available on lift chairs.
