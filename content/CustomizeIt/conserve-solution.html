Saving the planet, without sacrificing comfort

Exclusively from La-Z-Boy, conserve™ Sustainable Fabrics are made from recycled water bottles, yet provide the same comfort and style as traditional fabrics.

Step 1

Bottles are sorted, washed and chopped into flake.

Step 2

The flake is blended, melted and turned into chip.

Step 3

Versatile fiber is extruded from the recycled chip.

Step 4

The fiber is used for conserve Sustainable Fabrics.
