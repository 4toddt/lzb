Limitless Leather

It’s hard to top leather. From rich and refined to sleek and modern, our leather collection has a style and fit for everyone.

Reasons to love leather

Outlasts fabric 4 to 1

Leather is 50% stronger than other upholstery and has a life up to 15-20 years.

Year round comfort

Leather naturally breathes so it stays cool in the summer and warm in the winter.

Extremely durable

Natural oils prevent cracking, flaking, peeling and tearing, even along seam lines.

Ages beautifully

With age leather becomes softer while still retaining its shape and vibrance.

Color stays vibrant

Finished with special dyes to create a deep, rich color throughout the life of the leather.

Easy to care for

Simply requires a mild dusting or wiping to keep it looking vibrant for years to come.

Leather Types

With so many leather options, we want to help choose what’s right for you. Here’s a look at the basics to help you find your perfect type.

Authentic leather

More than 10 different tanning processes result in this elegantly supple, but durable leather. Pure, transparent dyes bring out its unique characteristics and dramatic color. If you prefer classic styles or more contemporary designs, authentic leather only improves with age.

Aniline Leather

Perfectly imperfect, this handcrafted, premium leather is treated with Aniline dye to preserve and enhance its natural, rustic beauty. Made in America, this high-quality leather has natural markings and variations in the grain, making each upholstered piece one-of-a-kind.

Select Leather

Chosen by hand for their texture and comfort, Select Leathers make everyone feel at home. This limited collection of genuine leather matched with vinyl urethane is both beautiful and budget friendly.

Custom Leather

Expertly suited to your needs and style, with genuine leather matched with leather-like vinyl urethane. Custom Leathers come in a wide variety of options and colors.

Custom Leather Plus

Elegant as well as comfortable, our genuine leather has an ageless quality and comes in a wide selection of rich colors and subtle textures.
