Calming Colors

Wallflower colors run the gamut from muted blues and slate grays to pinks, mauves and mustard. Incorporate the same tone throughout the room, or experiment with accent colors on chairs and pillows. Patterns that combine Wallflower colors create a subtle excitement. Accessorize with flowers and cozy throws for a look that’s peaceful, serene and oh-so-chic.
