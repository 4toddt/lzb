Meant to be mixed

Don’t be afraid to combine classic styles, like a button-tufted sofa or turned-leg table, with more contemporary ones. Keeping larger pieces neutral makes it easy to add a pattern or pop of color for a look that’s bold but not overpowering. Bring in a bit of boho with natural wood accents, organic prints and stylish planters.

Layered looks

It’s amazing how one chair can change a room, especially when it combines a statement making print with vibrant hues. Layering your sofa with complementing accent pillows adds texture and helps pull the look together. For a quick and easy style swap, introduce new wall art or lamps and place fragrant candles in seasonal colors.
