BEDROOM

Bold patterns & colors

Geometric patterns and bright colors add visual dimension to any room. Functional wood side tables add warmth and texture.

VIBRANT PATTERNS

Mixing vibrant patterns can anchor a small space. Classic features like metal, subtle wood grain and neutrals can bring an eclectic feel and balance.

DEN

COLORFUL & COZY

Refresh your living space with accent pillows, colorful lamps and textured trunks to bring that one-of-a-kind feel to your family room. Create a comfortable and cozy, yet "designer" type look!

BOLD COLOR

One wall in a bold color can offset neutral pieces and make the eye travel upward. Consider hanging colorful and fun artwork vertically versus horizontally.

DINING ROOM

Clean lines & texture

Clean lines, rich texture and unexpected details create a rich yet functional dining space! Keeping the space mostly neutral while adding pops of color creates balance.
