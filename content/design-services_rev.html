What to expect

Consultation

Whether you want a complete room transformation or just need a little advice, our design experts can help. Work with a professional in store, or schedule a free consultation and we’ll come right to you.

Collaboration

No one knows what you love and how you live better than you. That’s why we listen. Your thoughts and feedback are the inspiration behind our design, down to the very last detail.

Presentation

After collaborating with you on your project ideas and budget, you’ll receive a customized design plan so you can visualize your finished space. From colors to fabrics and more, see your design take shape.

Decoration

Watch the room come together, or wait for the reveal! Your designer will handle every detail, from overseeing the white-glove delivery and placement of your furniture, to styling and accessorizing.
