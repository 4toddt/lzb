La-Z-Boy Designer Danielle

Designer Danielle transformed her client's multi-level home on a back country road north of Pittsburgh. They wanted to update their rarely used great room and make it a space that functioned for their family.

The opportunity

The great room has several walkways and floor changes that limited our options for TV and furniture placement. The goal was to give the space a new look while maintaining the home’s rustic vibe. A recently installed stone accent wall set the tone for our design.

Leo Ottoman

Leighton Sofa

Barrow Chairside Table

The experience

The family trusted all our recommendations, including our ideas for paint, windows and floors. They were surprised by all the options we provided, and have even come back to work on other rooms.

The result

The client loved the finished look and have started using the room a lot more! There were many items they wanted to keep, and we made sure to work around them. The personal touches, including their antiques and amazing stone accent wall really made the room feel cozy!

“The family wanted to maintain the rustic feel of their home, so I blended a contemporary color palette with traditional elements.”
