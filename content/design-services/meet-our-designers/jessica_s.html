Q & A

Get to know our experts

What tips do you have for clients who are looking to re-design their space?

Go for comfort first! Once you find what’s comfortable for you, we have tons of flexibility to suit your style. We offer so many options - that’s the best part about La-Z-Boy! Also buy for you and don’t try to convince yourself to like something. No matter how attractive the design is, if you don’t love it or you can’t live in it day-to-day, then why do it?

What is your favorite part of designing a client’s homes?

Without a doubt, their reaction when they realize that everything we’ve been working on has captured exactly what they want. The reaction is a combination of awe, relief, and bliss. I love that moment and it’s the most rewarding feeling in the world knowing that I was the one they trusted to get them there.

What is your favorite way to spend your Saturday?

I’m a northern girl so I love doing anything outside- hiking, biking, kayaking, I’ll even take a walk around the river. Just getting away from everything to decompress works wonders!
