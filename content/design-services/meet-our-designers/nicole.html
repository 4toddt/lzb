Q & A

Get to know our experts

What intrigues you most about interior design?

I have always been intrigued by the process of transforming a space from concept to completion. I love watching my designs come to life and the excitement from my clients when they see their finished new room.

What common questions do you get from your clients regarding the design process?

Clients often worry that if they use a designer they will get a room the designer wants. I reassure them that my goal is to create their dream room, and that our design process ensures we meet their wants and needs.

Name one thing on your bucket list, and why.

Traveling to Italy. I want to see the architecture and art I studied in school firsthand while experiencing the food, culture and landscape.
