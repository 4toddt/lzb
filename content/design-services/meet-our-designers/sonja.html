Q & A

Get to know our experts

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I spend a good amount of time with each client just chatting about what they are dreaming about for their home. Style is so personal and individual and specific to each client. It goes beyond what something simply looks like. It’s really a way you live, and what you want your home to reflect.

Where do find your inspiration?

My inspiration comes from the people that I work with. I am inspired by their goals and vision, and what they want their home to be. When I get stuck, I use sites like Houzz and Instagram to move me outside of my box.

What aspect of your job brings you the greatest joy?

I love being a part of improving the lives of the people I work with. Home is the place where memories are made, experiences are had and relationships are built. It is a privilege to be a part of that impact.
