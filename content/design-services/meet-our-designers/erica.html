Q & A

Get to know our experts

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I try to listen to their likes and dislikes, what they are drawn to (and put off by!), and read between the lines of what they are not always able to articulate. I strive to create not only beautiful, but functional spaces.

What intrigues you about interior design and where do you find inspiration?

I love to travel as often as I can, and can find design inspiration anywhere. I am a lover of all things beautiful.

Name one thing on your bucket list, and why.

My number one bucket list item currently, is to visit Greece. I would love to take in the culture, food, architecture and beautiful geography.
