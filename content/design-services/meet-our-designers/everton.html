Q & A

Get to know our experts

How do you determine the right style for your clients?

My inspiration for a room often comes from taking things a client already has and incorporating them into their design. Designing the perfect room comes down to finding out what gives you joy and making those things visible in your home.

Where do you find your inspiration?

Inspiration is everywhere, you just have to pay attention to your surroundings. You’ll be amazed at the potential wealth of designs you can extract from even the tiniest items in your home.

Name one thing on your bucket list, and why.

I would like to visit every National Park. It’s just something I’ve always wanted to do!
