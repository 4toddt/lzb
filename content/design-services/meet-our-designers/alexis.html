Q & A

Get to know our experts

How do you determine the right style for your clients?

I first look at their home and the things they want me to work with. Asking them for pictures of things they love is also a great way to get inspiration. When buying furniture make sure you know the scale and size appropriate for your space. New pieces will not look good if they don’t fit the space!

What intrigues you about interior design and where do you find inspiration?

Every day is different! Every client, every space and every need is unique, so I cater my designs to the project at hand. I also love seeing how a space can be transformed with a new layout and color palette. For most projects, inspiration comes from something the client owns or asks me to work with. I’m also inspired by fashion, travel and fellow designers.

What’s your ultimate bucket list trip?

To be on a sailboat off the coast of Santorini, Greece while eating lots of bread, olives and cheese and drinking a crisp white wine.
