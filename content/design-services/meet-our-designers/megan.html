Q & A

Get to know our experts

What is a common concern you get from customers regarding the design process?

One of the most common questions I get during the design process is about color and pattern matching. “Would you match these things?” A lot of people are afraid of color and pattern but those are the things that make a room spectacular! Even in a monochromatic space, patterns are the driving force. Sometimes, having colors and patterns that differ drastically are exactly what someone needs.

What tips do you have for customers looking for new furniture?

Do some research ahead of time. Find some inspiration pieces and start a Pinterest board! This will help you down the line because you will have a better idea of what you like and don’t like.

What is your favorite way to spend a Saturday?

I love thrift shopping. I’ve made it a game to try and visit every hole in the wall or flea market I can find locally…and even some in the surrounding states!
