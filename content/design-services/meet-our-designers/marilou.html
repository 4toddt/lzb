Q & A

Get to know our experts

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I ask specific questions that explore their style. Also, by showing a variety of fabrics, and furniture silhouettes, I can get a better understanding of the design direction.

Where do find your inspiration?

Visiting landscape gardens, nature trails, and museums are great creative sources. I also watch HGTV and indulge with some great interior design magazines.

What’s one thing on your bucket list?

I’d like to get back to taking piano lessons. I have always felt that piano music brings joy into the home.
