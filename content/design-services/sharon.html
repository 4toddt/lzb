About Sharon

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I listen to what their vision is, and walk the showroom with them to see what furniture they like. Visiting their space can determine the style, especially if there are existing furnishings we are working with.

Where do you go to find inspiration?

I get my inspiration from everything — the cover of a magazine, the color on a wall, an accessory in your home, artwork, a pattern in a rug — there is so much beauty out there, you just have to look.

What is your favorite way to spend a Saturday, assuming you're not working?

When I am not visiting family and my precious grandchildren, my favorite way to spend a Saturday is touring unique places.
