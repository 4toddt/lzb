About Erin

What tips do you have for clients looking for a new furniture piece?

Evaluate your needs and determine what’s most important to you before starting the process. Trends and styles are constantly changing; it will help eliminate stress and confusion as you search for just the right piece.

What do you love about interior design?

From space planning to coordinating colors and finishes, I love the left and right brain aspects of interior design. Clients are always surprised that our design services are free, which makes it even more exciting to begin the process.
