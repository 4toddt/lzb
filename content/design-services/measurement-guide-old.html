Measurement Guide

Help us help you by measuring your space.

It will help us to tailor fit your new room.

step 1

Sketch outline of room

  • Sketch the outline of your room as if looking down from a birds eye view, as shown.
  • Include any windows, doors (display which way the doors open)
  • Show if there is a TV point in the room where it is located with (TV) on your sketch

step 2

Measure room

  • Measure overall length of each internal wall
  • Show each measurement of all windows, doors, main fixtures, i.e. fireplace as shown
  • Show all measurements in feet and/or inches

step 3

Take photos of room

  • Capture photos of room from the various angles as shown. This will allow us to get a good sense of your space
  • NOTE: Take photos of any furniture pieces that you would like to keep in the room.

step 4

Measure remaining furniture

  • Measure any furniture pieces you would like to keep in the room
