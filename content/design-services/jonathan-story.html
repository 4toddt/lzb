La-Z-Boy Designer Jonathan

Designer Jonathan helped a young couple who wanted a room with style, that could handle major entertaining and their beloved Great Dane. They live in a 3-bedroom home in a suburban development outside of Pittsburgh.

The opportunity

This couple has a large dog, so it was important to discuss fabric durability and space planning along with the design. Being open to the kitchen and dining room made this a great space for entertaining, and since they love red wine, iClean fabrics were a perfect touch.

Cosmopolitan Stationary Chair

Phoebe Sofa

Coronet Premier Ottoman

The experience

The couple had a vision but weren’t sure how to accomplish it. They loved that the 3D renderings showed the space to scale so they didn’t have to worry about how pieces fit or how colors would look.

The result

The couple wanted this space to be casual but with more of a regal punch, so they were thrilled to say the least! They were very excited to show off the new room to their friends and family. They were also shocked that La-Z-Boy offers this complimentary service.

“The couple was open to the whole design process. They were very trusting of my recommendations and ideas for their dream space.”
