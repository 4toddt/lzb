About Jill

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I ask lots of questions and listen to the answers. I’ve been told many times that I am a mind reader. But honestly, I really listen to my clients. From time to time I will have a client find pictures of rooms or items they love from the Internet. This is very helpful for the client and the designer.

Where do find your inspiration?

My personal style is what I call “organic contemporary.” I am inspired by nature — the desert, the forest, the ocean — and also by interesting architecture and fashion.

What aspect of your job brings you the greatest joy?

Helping people to improve their living spaces is very satisfying. We spend half our lives at home, so that space needs to be a retreat from the busy world. I absolutely love the look on the clients’ faces when they see their finished room and they love it.
