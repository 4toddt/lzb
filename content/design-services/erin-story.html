La-Z-Boy Designer Erin

Designer Erin visited the home of a newly single mom and lawyer living in North Scottsdale. Her client didn’t have time to decorate her new 3-bedroom home. She needed someone who could help give her and her boys a fresh start.

The opportunity

As a busy working mom, Erin's client was excited and relieved to hear about free Design Services. She described her previous home as more Tuscan/Traditional. She wanted to bring a little of that into the new design, but wanted a lighter more updated look for her open concept home.

The experience

The client immediately fell in love with the entire design and said “Yes” to everything! She was thrilled and relieved to have everything done, so that she could focus on her family and work.

The result

The client was speechless at the reveal! Over and over, she acknowledged how easy the process was. She didn’t have to think about little details and could instead focus on her personal and work related responsibilities. She was absolutely ecstatic with the outcome!

“She thought the final rooms were unique and breathtaking and adored the fact that I didn’t try to fit her style into a formula.”
