About Betsy

What is the design tip you just can’t stress enough?

Balance. Whether it’s balance in appropriate scale of furnishings or balance in quantity (or lack thereof), a balanced space creates beauty, harmony and an invitation to be in that space.

How do you determine the right style for your clients?

By asking my clients what they love and what they are drawn to. I also ask what’s not working in their home now. The right style can be chosen but most often we create something unique together.

What is your favorite way to spend your Saturday?

I have my go hiking days, my go shopping days and I have my cuddle my cat and read a book days.
