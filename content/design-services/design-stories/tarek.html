Tarek’s Story

La-Z-Boy Client

The opportunity

I’ve always wanted to have a personal designer but knew it could be costly. That’s when I discovered La-Z-Boy offers Free Design Services. I visited my local store and was shocked to find that they are truly a one-stop shop!

The experience

I was so impressed with the level of customer service and design expertise Erica provided. I gave her my color scheme with my budget and right away she had a vision. She also came to my home to ensure everything would fit and offered advice on accessories.

The result

It’s so beautiful, I never want to leave home! I love to entertain and get so many compliments. People cannot believe this is all from La-Z-Boy! I’ve already recommended Erica and La-Z-Boy to others and want to work with them on other rooms in my home.

Final thoughts

I wanted a home that would reflect my modern, eclectic style. Each piece is a statement in itself and my house the best house on the block. I can’t even express how much I love using this space. The possibilities are endless with La-Z-Boy.
