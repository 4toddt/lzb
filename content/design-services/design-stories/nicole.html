Nicole’s Story

La-Z-Boy Designer

The opportunity

The client was having difficulty finding the right pieces. They preferred more traditional looks, but had a new modern home. Those styles don’t always mesh well. They needed help finding items that would complement their home, but still be comfortable for their style.

The experience

Up to this point the client had been buying and returning items, so having a professional to help make decisions was key. They also appreciated that we were able to work with their existing pieces.

The result

We had the client leave while we set up, so they could experience the big reveal! They were so happy with the way it all came together and appreciated the thought we put into selecting each piece. It was the perfect blend of comfort and style for their home and life.
