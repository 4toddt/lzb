Design Story

Everton

La-Z-Boy Designer

The opportunity

I have worked with this family before. Since they had a great experience they came back to update their living room. They were tired of the design and wanted something different and fresh, but with two children, still needed it to be functional and family-friendly.

The experience

My client was open to all of my suggestions. I helped them choose everything from paint colors to window treatments, and even advised on a new fireplace design. The original fireplace was red brick.

The result

They couldn’t believe it was the same room! It looked just like they envisioned and they couldn’t believe that all the beautiful pieces were theirs to keep! The family couldn’t wait to show off their new room. They also recommended our service to neighbors and family.

Final thoughts

I help people find what inspires them, what gives them joy, and apply that to their design. This was the second room I had done for this family and since they had a great experience the first time, they came back for more.
