Cathy’s Story

La-Z-Boy Client

The opportunity

I just moved into this area and I wanted a fresh start. And of course I wanted new living room furniture. I didn’t know what color or what style, I didn’t really have a vision. I looked at a lot of furniture stores and didn’t find anything that I liked.

The experience

When I walked into the La-Z-Boy store, there was all kinds of possibilities. And the free design services is amazing. The designer came to my home and worked her magic. I met with her again and she had tables, lamps, artwork, She had it all. It was a lot of fun. She’s really great at her job.

The result

I love this room! This is probably my favorite spot in the whole house. It is very comfortable and it’s fun to have people come who haven’t been here yet so they can see my new home. It’s an inviting home to have!

Final thoughts

My designer had great suggestions for things I never dreamed I’d have in my new home. It was amazing to see it come together. I couldn’t be happier with the outcome and I’d definitely do it all over again.
