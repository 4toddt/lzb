Design Story

Susie & Greg

La-Z-Boy Clients

The opportunity

We moved into our home about two months ago. The thought of buying a house full of furniture is daunting. We had planned to hire a designer, since we knew we couldn’t do it all on our own. We went to La-Z-Boy for a couple of chairs and discovered their free design services.

The experience

Our designer was wonderful! She was able to give us the look we wanted, but couldn’t quite get on our own. We were also happy to see La-Z-Boy had office and bedroom furniture! They helped us achieve our vision of a beautiful home!

The result

Easily the most popular reaction we get is that our house should be in a magazine! Not only does it look nice, but it’s actually comfortable! I don’t think we could be happier with how it actually turned out.

Final thoughts

Our designer was informative, yet not pushy… and very patient with us. She helped us to find patterns and furniture that would fit perfectly in our home. I would recommend this service to anyone looking to buy or replace furniture.
