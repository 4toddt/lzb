Design Story

Erica’s Story

La-Z-Boy Client

The opportunity

Our old furniture was dated, oversized and worn; it just needed to go. I decided that I wanted La-Z-Boy because it’s good quality.

The experience

As I started looking around the store, I thought “Wow, everything is beautiful". I felt overwhelmed by all the great choices and I couldn’t do it on my own. The designer was free, why not just go for it. And it was so easy to use - that surprised me the most.

The result

Once I saw the design it blew my mind! The designer had ideas that I wouldn’t even of thought of. I can’t believe all the things that she could do in this little square room. When we want to be together, we always grab a spot in here; we don’t even use the other rooms.

Final thoughts

My designer listened to my vision, asked questions, added her input, and voila! I now have a beautifully designed room. It’s warm, welcoming, practical, useful…almost like an art piece. I will definitely do it again and I already know which room I’m doing next!
