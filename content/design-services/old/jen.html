About Jen

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I ask open-ended questions to get them to describe things to me instead of giving one-word answers. The more insight I receive about anything from their hobbies and leisure, the better I understand what the best fit for them is. I also look at fabrics, especially patterns, because the customer reveals their style through their pattern selections, giving me a more concrete view of their taste.

What is your favorite way to spend a Saturday, assuming you're not working?

My favorite way to spend a Saturday is with my family in nature. We love hiking, camping, fishing and canoeing. I am always seeing elements and colors and thinking, “How can I incorporate that into my space?”

What’s one thing on your bucket list?

One thing on my bucket list is to visit every national park in the U.S. and Canada. I am never disappointed with how nature and wildlife express balance, harmony and co-existence with man and nature. It is exciting to see colors from store samples pop up in unexpected places in nature.
