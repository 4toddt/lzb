About Jonathan

What tips do you have for clients who want to re-design their space?

Comfort is first! The best way to determine a unique style is to first find what fits with the most comfort. Once we find a comfortable style we can move toward style options and everything will fall into place. Designing the room of their dreams is why I do what I do! I love the reaction of the client when they are just in awe of their new room.

Why interior design?

When I was young I was always interested in helping my mom change her living room around or helping her make any remodeling decisions. Being a creative person I have been fascinated with anything in the design world. To design an amazing space with lots of textures and is visually appealing is what I strive for.

What is your favorite way to spend your Saturday?

Typically I am playing some type of intramural sport in our local sports league or enjoying brunch with friends.
