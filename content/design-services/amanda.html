About Amanda

How do you determine the right style for your customers?

I start by having a conversation with my customers to see if they use any design style buzzwords. Then I make sure that we have the same definition of the terms. I take clues from the types of furniture styles they prefer, as well as colors and patterns that they are drawn to. Another great indicator is the way they have designed other areas of their home, or even individual furniture or decor pieces that they love.

What aspect of your job brings you the greatest joy?

My favorite part is definitely the delivery. It is so fun to see everything come together in the space. I really enjoy getting to work on projects from start to finish!

What is your favorite way to spend a Saturday, assuming you're not working?

On my days off, I like to be outdoors. When it is nice outside, I go explore hiking trails. If it rains or is cold and nasty out, I will curl up with a blanket and a good book or Netflix.
