About Shannon

What’s your #1 design tip?

The design tip I cannot stress enough is that when combining patterns to make sure that you keep in mind to have different scales of fabrics. I have a lot of clients that like all different kinds of patterns but want to make sure they can combine them. I tell them they can as long as they vary in scale. You can have a large pattern on a chair but then a smaller pattern on a rug and vice-versa; as long as you don’t have multiple patterns that hold the same weight in scale.

What is your favorite part of designing a client’s home?

My favorite part is making their house into a home. I love helping them bring their wants and needs to life. I also love seeing their reactions when I can make their dreams come true.

Where do you find your inspiration?

I find my inspiration from what is around me. I can find it from a piece of art or nature and even fashion. I find inspiration from all over. The world is all color and design so it is really easy to find my inspiration anywhere.
