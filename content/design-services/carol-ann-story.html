La-Z-Boy Designer Carol-Ann

Carol-Ann's design expertise helped this couple add beauty and function to their California home that is nestled in a quiet neighborhood.

The opportunity

They wanted to update four rooms in their new home, with the goal of creating a comfortable and inviting environment they could enjoy with their family, pets and friends. The one requirement they had for this particular room was integrating their display case.

Moscato Stationary Chair

Talbot Sofa

Sunset Valley Cocktail Table

The experience

The client appreciated that the process was stress-free and easy. She was able to integrate the pieces she wanted to keep, and her vision was brought to life with little effort on her part.

The result

They had a house warming party right after we finished. Seeing everything come together was so exciting for everyone! They loved the cohesiveness of the design and the feeling of comfort throughout their new home. They were pleased with the whole experience.

“They were one of my most easy-going clients. They trusted the La-Z-Boy name and my design expertise.”
