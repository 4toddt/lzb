Comfort Coils

La-Z-Boy Mattresses feature a sophisticated array of fabric-encased pocket coils, which are designed to hold their shape and provide a consistent sleep surface. The coils along the perimeter are heavy gauge to prevent that “roll-off” feeling, while the coils in the center are pre-compressed, for an even surface that absorbs partner movement and prevents sleepers from rolling together.

State-of-the-Art Mattress Foam

La-Z-Boy is proud to offer a unique combination of advanced mattress foam for unsurpassed resiliency, support and all-night comfort.

Polyurethane Foam

All La-Z-Boy Mattresses include two layers of medium density (32 ILD) polyurethane foam — a 1” base layer and a 1” transitional layer between the coils and the memory foam.

Cool Flow Viscoelastic Memory Foam

Available on our Essential Mattresses, Cool Flow Viscoelastic Memory Foam relaxes quickly in response to body heat, providing greater comfort and support, enhanced air flow, superior heat and moisture dissipation and improved pressure distribution.

Copper Viscoelastic Memory Foam

Available on our Select Mattresses, Copper Viscoelastic Memory Foam improves upon the Cool Flow Viscoelastic Memory Foam by providing antimicrobial properties and greater heat dissipation for a cooler sleep experience.

Energex Temperature Responsive Foam

Available on our Select Mattresses, Energex Temperature Responsive Foam is a highly adaptive, durable and breathable foam specifically designed to react in response to body heat. Compared to standard memory foam, Energex recovers more quickly and provides significantly better pressure mapping.

State-of-the-Art Materials

All La-Z-Boy Mattresses are covered with soft and durable iClean™ performance fabric that repels liquids and stops spills from turning into stains. With iClean, spills bead up to be blotted away, making them perfect for households with children and pets.

Commonly Asked Questions

Do I need to select soft, medium or firm?

No. La-Z-Boy Mattresses flex and conform to the contours of your body, so it is not necessary to choose a firmness.

Do I need a box spring?

No. La-Z-Boy Mattresses work independently on a platform bed or with a box spring on a bed frame. The choice is yours.

How do you fit a mattress into a box?

La-Z-Boy Mattresses are designed to be compressed, folded and rolled for convenient home delivery.

What if I don’t like my new mattress?

We’re so sure you’ll like La-Z-Boy Mattresses that we’ll let you try one for 100 nights. If you’re not completely satisfied, call us to request a full refund. We’ll even pay for the return shipping.
