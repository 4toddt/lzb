Frequently Asked Questions

La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries offers customers a credit card through Synchrony Bank with a revolving credit limit to use again and again at la-z-boy.com and La-Z-Boy Retail locations.*

You can apply for a La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries Credit Card by applying online or at any of our participating retailer locations. Click here to apply online or here to find your nearest participating store location.

To check the status of your La-Z-Boy Furniture Galleries Credit Card account, click here to go to the Synchrony Bank website. After logging into your account, you will be able to:

  • Make a payment
  • View your balance
  • Update your account information
  • Schedule up to 12 future payments

There are three convenient ways to pay your bill.

Pay Online: and make payments online.

Pay by Phone: Contact Synchrony Bank Customer Service 1-844-335-5909 during the hours listed below to make a payment by phone.

Monday – Sunday 7:30 am to 12:00 midnight EST

Pay by Mail: Mail payments to the address below. Ensure that you have plenty of time for your payment to arrive prior to the due date.

Synchrony Bank
PO Box 960061
Orlando, FL 32896-0061

*Qualifying purchase amount must be on one receipt. No interest will be charged and equal monthly payments are required on promo purchase until it is paid in full. These payments equal the total promo purchase amount divided by the number of months in the promo period and rounded up to the next whole dollar. These payments may be higher than the payments that would be required if this purchase was a non-promo purchase. Regular account terms apply to non-promo purchases. For new accounts: Purchase APR is 29.99%. Minimum interest charge is $2. Existing cardholders: See your credit card agreement terms. Subject to credit approval.
