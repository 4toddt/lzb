Ready to see how La-Z-Boy furniture will look in your home? Thanks to augmented reality (AR), you can now get an instant sneak preview right on your phone.

How it Works

Step 1

Go to the product page of the furniture you’d like to see in your room.

Step 2

Customize the piece with your choice of fabric, leather or welt trim.

Step 3

Tap “View Virtually in Your Space” to see the product appear in your home.

Step 4

Follow the on-screen instructions to begin your AR experience.

Step 5

Snap a photo to share with friends or go back to the product page to purchase online.
